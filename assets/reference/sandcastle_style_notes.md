# Sandcastle Style Notes

Sandbox Moto should borrow the emotional warmth of sandcastle-style creation:

- The player shapes a small world by hand.
- The materials feel soft and tactile.
- Imperfection is charming.
- The scene feels sunny, safe, and imaginative.

Translate that feeling into motocross:

- Jumps are hand-shaped dirt mounds.
- Tracks look drawn through sand.
- Toy bikes are tiny and colorful.
- Props are oversized and playful.

Avoid realistic erosion, harsh machinery, or serious construction-site tone.
