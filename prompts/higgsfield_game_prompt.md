# Higgsfield Game Prompt

Create cozy Sandcastle-like visuals for Sandbox Moto, a nostalgic toy motocross sandbox imagination game about being a kid again, building miniature motocross tracks in dirt or sand, then watching toy bikes come alive.

The image should feel like someone is kneeling beside a warm sandbox. A handmade miniature motocross track runs through golden sand with sculpted berms, whoops, rollers, tabletops, doubles, triples, ruts, tire marks, worn jump faces, sand pushed out of corners, and little roost piles.

Show tiny fictional 1:12 toy motocross bikes racing through the track. The bikes have glossy molded plastic bodywork, chunky tires, simplified engines, oversized fenders, plastic number plates, colorful fictional graphics, tiny scratches, sandy tires, and no real-world motorcycle branding or exact real model designs.

World details:

- Oversized grass blades.
- Red bucket.
- Plastic shovel and rake.
- Popsicle-stick fences.
- Cones and flags.
- Toy dump trucks.
- Old tires.
- Plastic tunnels.
- Cardboard ramps.
- Toy spectators: army men, dinosaurs, block-style figures, stuffed animals, rubber ducks, plastic animals, and toy trucks.
- Warm golden light and soft shadows.
- Handmade diorama scale.

Include playful imagination bubbles:

- "Blue bike almost cleared the triple!"
- "Red bike loved that berm."
- "Green bike got stuck in the sand again."
- "One more race?"

Optional memory element: a Polaroid-style scrapbook card with a kid-style track name like "Mega Jump Raceway" or "The Impossible Triple."

Avoid gritty realism, pro racing presentation, stadiums, licensed motorcycles, real logos, serious telemetry, business UI, and simulator energy.

Communicate the loop: build -> race -> imagine feedback -> adjust -> one more race.
