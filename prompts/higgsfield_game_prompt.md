# Higgsfield Game Prompt

Create a Sandcastle-like cozy sandbox visual prototype for Sandbox Moto, a nostalgic motocross imagination game about being a kid again, building miniature motocross tracks in dirt or sand, then watching toy bikes come alive.

The image should feel like being a kid kneeling beside a warm backyard sandbox or dirt pile. A miniature motocross track is sculpted through golden sand with handmade dirt mounds, berms, whoops, rollers, a tabletop, a double, and a triple. Tiny fictional toy motocross bikes are racing on the track as if they have come alive through imagination.

The bikes should be 1:12 scale toy-style dirt bikes with glossy molded plastic bodywork, chunky tires, simplified engines, oversized fenders, plastic number plates, colorful fictional graphics, toy-like riders with oversized helmets, and no real-world motorcycle branding.

World scale should feel tiny and playful:

- Oversized grass blades around the sandbox edge.
- A red plastic bucket.
- A plastic shovel and rake.
- Popsicle sticks used like track markers.
- Tiny cones and flags.
- A toy dump truck.
- Pebbles that feel like mountains.
- A garden hose in the background.
- A fence or backyard edge far behind the sandbox.
- Real-world props that make the track feel tiny.

Mood and lighting:

- Warm golden sand.
- Soft sunny shadows.
- Cozy handcrafted diorama.
- Relaxed tactile building mood.
- Nostalgic childhood motocross imagination.
- Warm handcrafted diorama, not gritty realism.

Include subtle imagination feedback bubbles, handwritten or playful:

- Blue bike almost cleared the triple!
- Red bike loved that berm.
- Green bike got stuck in the sand again.
- Make the whoops smoother.
- That finish jump was awesome.
- I want to race again!

Avoid gritty realism, pro motocross stadiums, serious racing simulation, dark garage menus, sponsor-heavy visuals, mud-heavy realism, business management UI, and real motorcycle logos or exact real model designs.

The final result should communicate: Play Time / Build Track -> Press Play -> Sandbox comes alive -> Toy bikes race -> Imagination feedback appears -> Everything becomes still again -> Adjust track -> Race again.
