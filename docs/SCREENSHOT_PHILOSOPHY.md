# Screenshot Philosophy

Players should want to share builds because they look like tiny handcrafted memories.

## Photo Mode Ideas

- Golden hour.
- Tilt-shift.
- Polaroid frames.
- Scrapbook captions.
- Toy photography feel.
- Easy sharing later.

Screenshots should show the whole fantasy: tiny toy bikes, warm sand, handmade track features, oversized props, and the feeling that the sandbox came alive for one race.
