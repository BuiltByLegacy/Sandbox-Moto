# Unlock Philosophy

Never unlock power. Unlock imagination.

Progression should feel like finding new toys, not becoming stronger, richer, or more competitive.

## Unlock Examples

- Toy bikes.
- New colors.
- New props.
- New shovels.
- Buckets.
- Molds.
- Environments.
- Decorations.
- Stickers.
- Spectators.
- Scrapbook pages.
- Photo frames.

## Avoid

- Faster paid bikes.
- Prize money.
- Sponsor contracts.
- Business growth.
- Power upgrades that make old toys obsolete.
