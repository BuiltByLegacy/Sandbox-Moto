# World Bible

Locations are memories, not traditional maps.

Each world should include mood, lighting, sound, props, dirt/sand type, and unlocks.

## Backyard Sandbox

Mood: Safe, sunny, familiar.
Lighting: Warm afternoon.
Sound: Birds, wind, dog barking, distant kids.
Props: Red bucket, shovel, hose, grass, fence, popsicle sticks.
Dirt/sand: Warm golden sandbox sand.
Unlocks: Bucket colors, shovel shapes, toy dump truck, flags.

## Garage Floor In Winter

Mood: Cozy indoor play while outside riding is impossible.
Lighting: Cool window light with warm garage bulbs.
Sound: Garage radio, heater hum, muffled wind.
Props: Cardboard ramps, boots, bins, snow outside.
Dirt/sand: Tray sand, dust, cardboard.
Unlocks: Cardboard ramps, snow day bikes, garage stickers.

## Living Room Rug

Mood: Floor-play imagination.
Lighting: Soft indoor light.
Sound: Quiet house, TV in another room, footsteps.
Props: Blocks, pillows, toy bins, couch legs.
Dirt/sand: Rug fibers and toy ramps.
Unlocks: Block bridges, toy spectators, blanket hills.

## Driveway Dirt Pile

Props: Boards, weeds, hose, gloves.
Dirt/sand: Mixed dirt with small rocks.
