# Input Philosophy

Building should feel tactile, playful, and simple enough for a kid.

## Feel

Interaction should feel like dragging fingers through sand:

- Pull to raise.
- Push to flatten.
- Drag to smooth.
- Stamp to place.
- Scoop, pour, rake, and reshape.

Avoid CAD-like complexity, dense simulation panels, or precision-first editing.

## Platform Priority

- Mouse first for Steam.
- Steam Deck and controller later.
- iPad and touch later may be excellent for direct sandbox play.
- iPhone later should be simplified around smaller interactions.
