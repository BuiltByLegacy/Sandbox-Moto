# Toy Bike Bible

The bikes should look like collectible toy motocross bikes that came alive in a kid's imagination.

## Brand Rules

Use fictional toy brands only.

Do not use Honda, Yamaha, Kawasaki, KTM, Husqvarna, Suzuki logos, exact graphics, factory colorways, real rider gear, or model replicas.

## Visual Traits

- Chunky plastic bodywork.
- Glossy molded plastic.
- Simplified engines.
- Thick tires.
- Oversized fenders.
- Colorful fictional graphics.
- Plastic number plates.
- Tiny scratches and sand.
- 1:12 scale toy proportions.

## Behavior

Bikes animate like toys, not real motorcycles:

- Slight bounce while riding.
- Simple wheelies over jumps.
- Whole-bike tilt in air.
- Landing bounce.
- Tiny roost or sand spray.
- Gentle wobble or spinout crashes.

Avoid full realistic suspension simulation.
