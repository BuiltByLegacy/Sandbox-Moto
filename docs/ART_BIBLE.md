# Art Bible

## Visual Direction

Sandbox Moto should feel like a warm handmade miniature diorama:

- Sandcastle-inspired cozy sandbox look.
- Tiny Glade-inspired relaxed creation.
- Mini Motorways-inspired clean tool bubbles and editability.
- 1:12 scale toy motocross bikes.
- Warm golden sand.
- Soft shadows.
- Glossy molded plastic.
- Chunky toy-bike proportions.
- Oversized grass, buckets, shovels, cones, toy trucks, fences, old tires, and popsicle-stick markers.
- Handmade, tactile, toy-like, nostalgic.

## Avoid

- Gritty realism.
- Exact real motorcycle brands or licensed bikes.
- Pro racing presentation.
- Sponsor-heavy visuals.
- Dark garage menu aesthetics.
- CAD-like editor visuals.

## Materials

Sand and dirt should feel soft, shapeable, and played in. Toy bikes and props should have rounded plastic forms, glossy highlights, tiny scratches, and sandbox dust.

## Toy Crowd

Spectators should be toys: army men, dinosaurs, block-style figures, stuffed animals, rubber ducks, plastic animals, and toy trucks.
