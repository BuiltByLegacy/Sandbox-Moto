# Worlds And Locations

## Goal

Locations should make Sandbox Moto feel like tiny motocross worlds hidden inside everyday places.

The player should feel like they are kneeling beside a sandbox, dirt pile, garage floor, or pit area and letting imagination turn it into a full race track.

## Scale Language

Everything should feel tiny:

- Toy bikes.
- Toy riders.
- Oversized grass blades.
- Plastic shovel.
- Red bucket.
- Garden hose.
- Popsicle sticks.
- Rocks as mountains.
- Tiny cones.
- Toy dump truck.
- Fence in the background.
- Real motocross bikes heard far away.

## Possible Locations

### Backyard Sandbox

The core fantasy. Warm golden sand, grass blades around the edge, red bucket, plastic shovel, garden hose, small fence, and backyard afternoon ambience.

### Local Motocross Pits

A dirt pile beside trailers, gas cans, folding chairs, and distant real bikes. The toy track lives in the pit dirt while the real race world hums far away.

### Dirt Pile Behind The Garage

A rougher backyard dirt pile with boards, tools, garage siding, and weeds. Great for scrappy homemade tracks.

### Camping Weekend Beside The Camper

Sand and dirt near a camper, folding chairs, cooler, lantern, and evening crickets.

### Garage Floor In Winter

A cozy indoor map with cardboard ramps, spilled sand tray, toy bikes, garage radio, and snow visible outside.

### Edge Of A Practice Track

The toy track exists beside a real practice track. Real bikes are heard in the distance while the tiny world stays playful.

### Beach Sand

Soft sand, driftwood, shells, pails, and a more relaxed sunny palette.

### County Fairgrounds

Dusty dirt, temporary fences, tiny flags, food stand shapes in the distance, and warm evening light.

### Dad's Truck Tailgate

A compact diorama on a truck tailgate with dirt in a tray, straps, cooler, boots, and real motocross life around the edges.

### Snow Day Sandbox

A winter version with cold outside ambience, snow around the sandbox, and warm nostalgia from still playing with dirt and toy bikes.
