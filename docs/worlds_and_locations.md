# Worlds And Locations

## Goal

Locations are memory worlds, not traditional maps. Each place should feel like a remembered play scenario where a kid's imagination turns ordinary dirt, sand, carpet, snow, or a pit-area corner into a full motocross world.

The player should feel like they are kneeling beside a sandbox, dirt pile, garage floor, or pit area and letting imagination turn it into a full race track.

## Scale Language

Everything should feel tiny:

- Toy bikes.
- Toy riders.
- Oversized grass blades.
- Plastic shovel.
- Red bucket.
- Garden hose.
- Popsicle sticks.
- Rocks as mountains.
- Tiny cones.
- Toy dump truck.
- Fence in the background.
- Real motocross bikes heard far away.

## Memory Locations

### Backyard Sandbox

Mood: The core fantasy. Warm, safe, sunny, and familiar.

Props: Oversized grass, red bucket, plastic shovel, rake, garden hose, tiny cones, popsicle sticks, fence.

Ambient sound: Birds, wind, distant kids, dog barking, faint neighborhood sounds.

Sand/dirt type: Warm golden sandbox sand that shapes easily.

Unlockable toy items: Bucket colors, shovel shapes, toy dump truck, flags, plastic tunnels.

### Garage Floor In Winter

Mood: Cozy indoor play while it is too cold to ride outside.

Props: Cardboard ramps, spilled sand tray, toy bikes, boots, snow outside the garage door, storage bins.

Ambient sound: Garage radio, heater hum, muffled wind, distant house sounds.

Sand/dirt type: Tray sand, cardboard, and dusty concrete.

Unlockable toy items: Cardboard ramps, plastic cones, garage radio sticker set, snow day bike variants.

### Living Room Rug

Mood: Imagination on the floor with toys spread everywhere.

Props: Rug fibers like tall terrain, couch legs, toy bins, pillows, books, blocks.

Ambient sound: Quiet house ambience, TV in another room, footsteps upstairs.

Sand/dirt type: No true dirt; carpet ridges and toy ramps act like terrain.

Unlockable toy items: Block bridges, plastic tunnels, toy spectators, blanket hills.

### Driveway Dirt Pile

Mood: Scrappy backyard engineering with a pile of dirt that becomes a race track.

Props: Driveway edge, boards, weeds, hose, work gloves, garage siding.

Ambient sound: Wind, neighborhood cars, distant mower, dog barking.

Sand/dirt type: Mixed backyard dirt with small rocks.

Unlockable toy items: Mini excavator, PVC pipes, rocks-as-mountains, driveway chalk markers.

### Local Motocross Pits

Mood: Real motocross energy heard from far away while the toy world stays small and safe.

Props: Trailers, folding chairs, gas cans, boots, tie-down straps, pit mats, pickup trucks.

Ambient sound: Distant real dirt bikes, PA muffled far away, tools, people talking.

Sand/dirt type: Pit dust and packed dirt.

Unlockable toy items: Pit cones, tiny flags, toy campers, tailgate props.

### Tailgate At Race Day

Mood: A compact dirt tray or tailgate setup while race day happens around the edges.

Props: Truck tailgate, cooler, boots, straps, helmet, gloves, folding chair.

Ambient sound: Distant bikes, tailgate creaks, wind, muffled crowd.

Sand/dirt type: Dirt tray, loose pit soil, and dust.

Unlockable toy items: Tailgate ramps, strap barriers, cooler stickers, race day bike variants.

### Camping Trip

Mood: Evening campground play after riding or exploring all day.

Props: Camper, folding chairs, cooler, lantern, fire ring, pine needles.

Ambient sound: Crickets, wind in trees, campground voices, distant bike unloading.

Sand/dirt type: Dry campground dirt mixed with pine needles.

Unlockable toy items: Toy campers, lanterns, camp cones, pinecone obstacles.

### Edge Of A Practice Track

Mood: The tiny imagined track sits beside the real one, like a kid making a miniature version.

Props: Practice track fence, grass edge, stakes, tape, rocks, water hose.

Ambient sound: Real bikes far away, wind, flags snapping, water truck hum.

Sand/dirt type: Packed practice-track dirt and loose berm dust.

Unlockable toy items: Fence stakes, track tape, practice flags, water truck prop.

### Beach Vacation

Mood: Bright vacation play where beach sand becomes a motocross track.

Props: Shells, driftwood, pails, towels, beach grass, umbrella legs.

Ambient sound: Waves, gulls, wind, distant kids.

Sand/dirt type: Soft beach sand that slows bikes and collapses easily.

Unlockable toy items: Beach sand bike variants, shell markers, driftwood bridges, pail molds.

### Snow Pile

Mood: A winter imagination world where snow piles become jumps.

Props: Mittens, sled, shovel, snowbank, driveway edge, garage light.

Ambient sound: Muffled snow quiet, distant plow, boots crunching, garage radio.

Sand/dirt type: Snow, icy crust, and hidden gritty sand.

Unlockable toy items: Snow day variants, plastic sled ramps, mitten flags, snow tunnel.

### Toy Room

Mood: Pure toy-box imagination with props and ramps everywhere.

Props: Toy shelves, blocks, action figures, plastic trees, cardboard ramps, toy trucks.

Ambient sound: Quiet room tone, toy bin clacks, distant family sounds.

Sand/dirt type: Kinetic sand tray, carpet, cardboard, and plastic ramps.

Unlockable toy items: Toy trees, spectators, bridges, plastic tunnels, themed bike shelves.
